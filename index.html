Traditional House

It is a three hundred meter house in the heart of Qomsheh city. Qomsheh is a city that is at least seven hundred years old and today it is called Shahreza. The original house dates back to one hundred and fifty years ago, and of course it has changed every ten years with the storm of modernity. The house was bought about fifty years ago by Haj Agha Hedayat Jalal al-Dini and was rebuilt in 2018 by his son and grandson with the aim of introducing Shahreza pottery and accommodating the city's tourists.
